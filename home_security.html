Home Security

Your home is where you should feel safe.

CSL Security can design a home alarm system that provides your home and family with a level of protection you’re comfortable with. Alarm monitoring and response, CCTV cameras and panic alarms can all be included.We want to make sure that when your head hits the pillow or you take off on your family holiday, you know we’re there when you need us. And, even if you live by yourself, you’ll never be alone in an emergency.

Home Alarm Systems:

These can be as simple as a keypad and control panel (the brains of the system) a siren (speaker that screams!) and a couple of motion sensors (PIRs). This connected to our 24hr monitoring service will give you the ultimate peace of mind. From this base setup you can add the extras, remotes, wireless sensors for the shed, panic buttons, smoke detectors etc.

Smoke Detectors

Do you get tired of having to change the batterys in your smoke detectors and if they activate and no one is home what happens from there? If you have an alarm system you can have smoke detectors hard wired to it, so no more batteries to worry about but more importantly these included in a monitored home security system means we are looking over your property while you are asleep or on holiday.

Access Control

What is Access Control? This is the next generation in security systems. Its gives you total control of your property via the web. Open doors with keypads or swipe tags, view cameras, you can even control your house lighting with it. Give us a call to discuss how we can design and intergrate access control into your new build.

Driveway Beams

Do you want to know who is coming onto your property. Someone stealing your avocados? You may have a gate quite a distance from the house. No worries, we can fit a high quality (NZ made) solar powered (no hassle of running wires)beam set. If the beam is 'broken' this means someone is on your section and so it transmits back to a reciever base set that you can have located by the bed, in the kitchen, hallway, wherever there is standard plug connection.You will definetly hear it! These can be incorporated into you alarm system and they can even text you this message.

CCTV Systems

Keeping an eye on your property doesnt need to cost an arm and a leg! We have different options based on what you need to see and your budget. This technology has improved greatly in the last few years. The camera resolution is incredible and can be viewed from your smart phone if required. Have a record of who is coming onto your property while you’re at work or check up on the kids after school. Call us for a site visit and we can discuss the best option for you.

Panic Alarms

If you have elderly parents living alone or you want to feel safe at night in your house. A security system combined with a monitored panic pendant may be the answer. They can be worn on the wrist, as a pendant (great for out in the garden) or fixed by the bed. If the button is activated it sends a signal to CSL Security stating theres is an issue and we act immediatley upon it based on your request. Definite peace of mind.

Home Insurance
